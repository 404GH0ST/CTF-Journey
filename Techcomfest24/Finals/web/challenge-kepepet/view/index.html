Welcome to Death Note

Experience the power to determine the fate of others. With Death Note, you can write down the names of individuals and decide their destiny.

Use this app to:

  • Write the names of individuals and specify their cause of death.
  • Witness the mysterious and supernatural events that follow.
  • Unleash your inner Shinigami and play with the boundaries of life and death.

Warning: Use this app responsibly and ethically. Be careful what you wish for.
